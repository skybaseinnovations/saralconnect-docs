    Set up Your School

    Step 1: Add Teacher

    1. Go to Teacher
    2. Select New Teacher
    3. Enter details
    4. Click Save


    Step 2: Assign Subject and Teacher

    1. Go to Academics
    2. Select Assign Subject and Teacher to class 3.Click Add

    After that you can choose teacher, class and subject and click save.
